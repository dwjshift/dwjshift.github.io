Hi! I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the University of California, San Diego, advised by Prof. Hao Su. Before that, I completed my M.S. and B.S. at the Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University, where I was advised by Prof. Yang Gao and Prof. Chongjie Zhang.

My research interests lie in robot learning and reinforcement learning.
