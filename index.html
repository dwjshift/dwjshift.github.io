Hi! I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the University of California, San Diego, advised by Prof. Hao Su. Before this, I completed my M.Eng. and B.Eng. degrees at the Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University, where I was advised by Prof. Yang Gao and Prof. Chongjie Zhang.

My research lies at the intersection of robotics and machine learning. I am particularly interested in exploring how to develop scalable and general-purpose learning frameworks for high-reliability robotic manipulation in unstructured real-world environments. My previous research has also spanned reinforcement learning and imitation learning algorithms.
